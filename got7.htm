GOT7 dropped a full schedule for their comeback, 'Eyes On You'.


SEE ALSO: GOT7 now has 2 million likes on 'Just Right'


JYP Entertainment recently announced and confirmed that GOT7 will be making their comeback on March 12 and soon after, released a full tracklist for their upcoming album 'Eyes On You'. Today, they have dropped a full schedule that will help the fans keep track of all the teasers leading up to the full release. 


Some key dates include a pre-release of "Only You" featuring Hyorin on February 28, "Look" MV teaser on March 9, and of course, the full release of 'Eyes On You' album on March 12.
